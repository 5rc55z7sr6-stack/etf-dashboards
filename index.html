// Where the money is going — and how to be early

Stop watching prices. Watch the ratios.

The absolute price of any ETF tells you little. The relationship between two ETFs tells you the regime: risk-on or risk-off, growth or defense, reflation or fear. This desk maps the most liquid fund per sector — US and worldwide — against ~15 intermarket ratios that professional traders use to spot rotation before it completes.

MASTER GAUGE 01
XLY / XLP
Consumer risk appetite. Discretionary over staples = the economy is wanted.
MASTER GAUGE 02
HYG / LQD
Credit appetite. The most reliable risk gauge under stress — credit leads.
MASTER GAUGE 03
SPY / TLT
Stocks vs. long bonds — the classic risk-on / risk-off switch.
MASTER GAUGE 04
Copper / Gold
Growth vs. fear. Industrial demand against the safe haven.

Discipline: require ≥2 of the 4 master gauges to agree before sizing up. When they disagree — cut size, stand aside.

SECTION 03 / REGIME ARCHETYPES

Read them together

Score each lens +1 (rising / risk-on) or −1 (falling / risk-off) by its 50-day MA slope. Net the score across lenses to identify which of three regimes you're in.

REGIME A · NET POSITIVE

Risk-On / Reflation

Signals aligned
XLY/XLP, SMH/SPY, IWM/SPY, HYG/LQD, Copper/Gold, RSP/SPY all rising; SPY/TLT up.
Position
Long cyclicals, tech, semis, small caps, EM. Underweight bonds & gold. Press the leaders.
REGIME B · NET NEGATIVE

Risk-Off / Flight to Safety

Signals aligned
Offense/defense & credit ratios falling; SPY/TLT down; GLD/SPY up; VIX rising.
Position
Defensives (XLP, XLU, XLV), Treasuries (TLT/IEF), gold, cash. Protect capital.
REGIME C · MIXED / FRAGILE

Narrow / Late-Cycle

Signals diverge
Index (SPY/QQQ) rising but RSP/SPY & IWM/SPY falling; credit flat; Copper/Gold soft.
Position
Stay long the leaders but tighten stops. Do NOT add broad beta — this is fragility, not opportunity.

Murphy's business-cycle sequence: bonds turn before stocks, commodities turn last. A falling Copper/Gold and rising bonds while equities are still high is the classic late-cycle warning.

SECTION 04 / EXECUTION

The rotation playbook

Build the dashboard, define the signal, act on rotation — not prediction.

1

Build the core dashboard

Chart the 11 US SPDRs + IXN as the sector layer, then the 15 ratios. Put the four master gauges on a top regime panel. Use TradingView "XLY/XLP" ratio syntax with 50- & 200-day MAs. Trade the ratio's trend, not its level.

2

Define your regime signal

Score each lens +1 / −1 on its 50-day MA slope. Net positive → risk-on playbook (XLK, IXN, SMH, IWM, XLY). Net negative → defensive (XLP, XLU, XLV, TLT, GLD, cash). Require ≥2 of 4 master gauges to agree before sizing up.

3

Act on rotation

SMH/SPY or HYG/LQD rolling over while QQQ/SPY still rises = early de-risk (semis & credit lead). RSP/SPY turning up after a narrow market = broaden into cyclicals & small caps. EFA/SPY or EEM/EFA breaking up = add international / EM.

SPY monthly close < 200-DMAShift to defensive playbook (Faber / Antonacci regime filter).
HYG/LQD breaks 200-DMA downDe-risk regardless of index strength — credit leads equities.
Copper/Gold + 10Y yield roll over togetherMaterial slowdown signal — add duration & defensives.
RSP/SPY new lows while SPY new highsFragility — stop adding beta, tighten stops.
CadenceReview ratios daily, re-score regime weekly. Intraday only around Fed / CPI / catalysts.
SECTION 05 / RISK NOTES

Where this breaks

AUM moves with markets

GLD net assets were $155.1B (SEC 10-Q, 3/31/2026) up from $124.5B (9/30/2025) on the gold rally; IBIT is BTC-price-driven; XLK is reported ~$103–120B. Treat all AUM as magnitude, verify on issuer sheets before sizing.

Global sector funds are thin

MXI, KXI, RXI, JXI, IXP, EXI are small and thinly traded — context only, not active entries. Their precise AUM is unconfirmed; check iShares fact sheets.

Correlations are regime-dependent

The Copper/Gold–yield link has weakened sharply since ~2022. Stocks & bonds can fall together in inflation (breaking SPY/TLT). Always confirm with a second lens.

VIX products decay

VIXY / VXX suffer structural roll decay — tactical hedges only, never buy-and-hold.

No single ratio is a system

These are context & confirmation tools layered onto price action and risk management — consistent with how Murphy, StockCharts and All Star Charts frame intermarket work.

Forward calls are opinions

Any 2026 sector-outperformance projections in source material are opinions, not facts, and are excluded from the positioning logic here.
